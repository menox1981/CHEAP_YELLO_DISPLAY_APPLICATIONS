How it works

From browser tab to running firmware

No PlatformIO, no Arduino IDE, no drivers beyond what your browser already has.

🗂️

1. Pick an application

Browse the showcase and choose the app and variant that matches your board and budget.

🔌

2. Plug in over USB

Connect your Cheap Yellow Display, click Install, and choose its serial port when prompted.

✅

3. Done

The firmware flashes directly from the page. Reboot, connect to WiFi, and you're running.
